✨ About Padma

Padma Patil is a proud Ravenclaw, twin sister to Parvati Patil, and an overlooked gem in the wizarding world. Known for her intelligence, composure, and low tolerance for nonsense, Padma is often seen in the background—but she’s got main character energy if you pay attention.

She attended the Yule Ball with Ron Weasley (yikes), fought in the Battle of Hogwarts (iconic), and probably rolled her eyes at every dumb boy at Hogwarts (legend behavior).

💙 Personality Vibes

  • 🌀 Elegant but doesn't beg for attention
  • 🧊 Slightly intimidating in the best way
  • 🔥 Probably the friend everyone goes to for Real Talk™
